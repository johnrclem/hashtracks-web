**🔴 Year-inference gotcha — do NOT use a naive today-anchored bidirectional rollover.** The dates are
year-less `MM/DD`, and the 歷史足跡 section deliberately shows ~6 months of **past** runs (#2756 = 01/03
is ~5 months before today). A Bangkok-style "candidate >60 days in the past → refYear+1" rule would
push those Jan/Feb history rows a full year into the **future** — corrupting them. Anchor on the
**run-number → date relationship** instead (run numbers are reliable + strictly weekly):
1. Take the **本週活動 current run** as the reference: `refRun = #2779`, `refDate = parse("06/13", currentYear)`
   choosing the year that puts `refDate` nearest to *today* (±2 weeks).
2. For every other row, `expectedDate = refDate + (row.runNumber − refRun.runNumber) × 7 days`.
3. Resolve the row's year by picking `y ∈ {expectedYear−1, expectedYear, expectedYear+1}` that makes
   the row's `MM/DD` **closest to `expectedDate`** (validate each candidate via `Date.UTC` round-trip so
   an impossible date can't mask a valid sibling year). Store UTC noon.

This is exact for a weekly kennel and handles the Dec→Jan boundary and the deep history cleanly.
